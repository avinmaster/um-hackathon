06 / The Dependency Test
🔌

Remove GLM.
The system stops.

Not degrades. These five functions do not exist without GLM. There is no version that does less — only a version that does nothing.

✗No GLM → No template drafting. Admins face a blank editor. Shah Alam's workflow doesn't exist.
✗No GLM → No document reading. Uploaded files are binary blobs. Nothing is extracted. Nothing is known.
✗No GLM → No compliance check. Forms submit. Nothing ever verifiably passes or fails.
✗No GLM → No cross-check. The form says 10 floors. The plan shows 12. It publishes wrong.
✗No GLM → No visitor assistant. The chat panel loads. There's nothing for it to say.

The forms render. The buttons work. The platform does nothing.
