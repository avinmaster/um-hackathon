# Menara Demo — Public Listing Pack

This pack accompanies the public-facing listing for **Menara Demo**, a
strata-titled residential tower in Section 7, Shah Alam.

---

- **Floors above ground:** 18
- **Total residential units:** 96
- **Footprint:** 1,850 m²
- **Year completed:** 2014

## Amenities

- Infinity-edge swimming pool (level 7 podium, 25 m, lap-rated)
- Fully-equipped gymnasium with cardio and free-weights zones
- Multipurpose hall (Surau on level 1, capacity 60)
- Children's playground and reading corner
- 24-hour security with CCTV and access-card lifts
- Visitor parking: 24 bays
- EV charging stations: 4 (Type 2, 22 kW)
- Rooftop garden with seating

## Floor layout

The tower follows a centre-core layout with six units per typical
floor (level 8 through 18). Levels 7 and below house the lobby,
podium amenities, and parking. Service yards face the rear elevation;
all living spaces face north or east to minimise afternoon heat gain.

Cross ventilation is achieved through the core-and-corridor
configuration; each unit has at least two operable façades.

## Neighbourhood

Menara Demo sits two minutes' walk from Stesen LRT Shah Alam (line
extension), with Section 7 commercial belt and UiTM Shah Alam within
a 5-minute drive. The address — No. 12, Jalan Demo, Seksyen 7, 40000
Shah Alam — places it on the quieter inland side of the section.
