**Building name:** Menara Demo

**Floors:** 18 above ground, 1 basement
**Total units:** 96

## Inspection Findings

The above premises has been inspected and found to comply with the Fire
Services Act 1988 (Act 341) and the Uniform Building By-Laws 1984
(Selangor) in the following areas:

- Active fire protection: hose reels, sprinklers (NFPA 13), wet-riser
  system charged at 7 bar.
- Passive fire protection: fire-rated separation (2 hr) between units;
  protected staircases.
- Means of escape: two protected stairwells, total egress capacity
  meets occupancy requirements.
- Fire alarm: addressable system with annunciator panel at the ground
  floor lobby; tested December 2023.
- Emergency lighting and signage: compliant.

## Conditions

This certificate is valid only so long as no alterations are made to
the building's fire protection systems without prior approval of the
Director of Fire and Rescue, Selangor.
